Group Profile

Sachdeva Group Profile

The Sachdeva Group, through its companies Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breaking Unit) LLP and Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers Pvt. Ltd., has been a cornerstone in the ship recycling industry since its inception in Alang, Gujarat, in 1983. The Group operates on approximately 4,950 square meters of dedicated land, with the capacity to process over 100,000 lightweight tons of ships annually.

Our journey began under the name Madhav Industrial Corporation, which led the way in shipbreaking until 1997. In that year, we rebranded to Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breakers) and have successfully recycled 37 ships. To meet growing demand and further expand our operations, we established Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers Pvt. Ltd. in 1998, which has since recycled 36 ships.

Sachdeva Group places the utmost priority on environmental stewardship and occupational health and safety. Our ship recycling activities adhere to stringent local and international legal standards, including those set by the International Maritime Organization (IMO), the International Labour Organization (ILO), the Basel Convention, the Hong Kong International Convention, and the European Union Ship Recycling Regulation.

By combining decades of experience with a commitment to sustainable practices, Sachdeva Group continues to lead the way in responsible ship recycling

VISION

To be a global leader in sustainable ship recycling, dedicated to minimizing environmental impact, ensuring the highest standards of safety and health, and creating value through innovation and excellence. We aim to set new benchmarks in green ship recycling by integrating advanced technologies, fostering a culture of responsibility and integrity, and contributing to a circular economy for a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable future.

MISSION

Our mission is to lead the ship recycling industry by delivering safe, sustainable, and environmentally responsible solutions. We are committed to minimizing environmental impact, ensuring the highest standards of safety for our workforce, and adhering to international regulations and best practices. Through innovation, integrity, and a dedication to excellence, we aim to recycle ships responsibly, maximize resource recovery, and contribute to a greener, more sustainable future for the maritime industry.

OUR COMPANIES

Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breakers) LLP.

Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breaking Unit) has been a key player in the ship recycling industry since the inception of ship-breaking activities at Alang in 1983. Building on this legacy, we formally established our business under the name Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breakers) in 1994.

With a track record of recycling 37 ships to date, we are committed to delivering safe, sustainable, and efficient ship recycling services. Our company operates with a focus on environmental responsibility, compliance with international standards, and a dedication to excellence, making us a trusted name in the global ship recycling industry.

Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers PVT. LTD.

Sachdeva Steel Products (Ship Breaking Unit) LLP and Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers Pvt. Ltd. have been integral players in the ship recycling industry since the inception of ship-breaking activities at Alang in 1983. Our Group first operated under the name of Madhav Industrial Corporation and later transitioned to Sachdeva Steel Products in 1997. To expand our operations, we established Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers Pvt. Ltd. in 1998.

With a strong legacy and decades of expertise, Jai Jagdish Ship Breakers Pvt. Ltd. has successfully recycled 36 ships to date. We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, safety, and compliance with international standards, ensuring sustainable ship recycling for a greener future.
